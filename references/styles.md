# Style Presets

Paste-ready style blocks. Pick one (or let the user pick), then **append it after the
prompt-recipe preserve clause**. Each already sets materials + lighting + mood so you only
adjust the camera/environment if needed.

---

### 1. Modern minimalist (default exterior)
> Clean modern minimalist architecture. Materials: white plaster and exposed concrete, large
> floor-to-ceiling glass, matte black frames, slim metal railings. Bright midday sun, soft crisp
> shadows, clear blue sky. Landscaped lawn, a single sculptural tree, clean paved walkway.
> Eye-level three-quarter view.

### 2. Mediterranean / warm
> Warm Mediterranean residential style. Materials: cream stone and stucco walls, terracotta roof,
> natural wood shutters and pergola, stone paving. Warm late-afternoon sun, olive trees and
> bougainvillea, a calm courtyard. Inviting, sun-drenched mood.

### 3. Golden-hour exterior (best for marketing)
> Cinematic golden-hour lighting, warm low sun, long soft shadows, glowing warm tones on the
> facade. Glass reflecting the orange sky, interior lights just starting to glow. Lush landscaping,
> a few people for scale. Premium real-estate hero feel.

### 4. Blue-hour / night
> Blue-hour dusk, deep blue sky, building interiors glowing warm through the glass, soft exterior
> uplighting on the facade and landscaping. Wet reflective paving, subtle ambient city glow.
> Elegant, high-end nighttime architectural shot.

### 5. Interior daylight
> Photorealistic interior visualization. Bright natural daylight pouring through large windows,
> soft realistic shadows, warm wood floors, light walls, tasteful modern furniture, indoor plants.
> Wide-angle at standing eye height, cozy and airy.

### 6. Aerial / drone
> High aerial drone view of the building and its plot. Shows the roof, surrounding landscaping,
> driveway, neighboring context and street. Bright clear daylight, crisp shadows, realistic
> top-down-angled perspective. Great for masterplan / site context.

### 7. Rainy / moody cinematic
> Overcast rainy atmosphere, soft diffused light, wet reflective surfaces and paving, light mist,
> dramatic moody sky. Cool tones with warm glowing windows. Cinematic, editorial architecture mood.

### 8. Marketing hero shot
> Ultra-premium real-estate marketing render. Perfect golden lighting, immaculate landscaping,
> luxury detailing, pristine glass and stone, a beautiful sky, lifestyle scale figures.
> Wide low hero angle, aspirational and polished — billboard quality.

---

**Tip:** for a quick client option set, render the same input with #1, #3 and #4 to give
*midday*, *golden-hour* and *night* versions of the same building.
