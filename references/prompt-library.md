and append to the building description. They **preserve the original architecture** unless the user
asks otherwise. Compatible with Midjourney, DALL·E, Stable Diffusion, Flux, Ideogram, Leonardo, V-Ray,
Corona, Lumion, Twinmotion, Enscape, D5, Blender, SketchUp, Rhino, Revit, Archicad.
